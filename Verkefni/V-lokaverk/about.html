About the hotel:

Grand Hotel Reykjavík is a first class hotel for business travellers, conference guests and tourists, rated a luxurious 4-star hotel. It has 314 rooms which are very large and equipped with first class amenities. Rooms with facilities for disabled persons are also available, and ajoining rooms (walk-through) are available upon request. For business guests, all rooms have a wireless internet connection. Room service is available 24 hours a day. It is near the centre of Reykavik, in a quiet area within a short distance from all services.
